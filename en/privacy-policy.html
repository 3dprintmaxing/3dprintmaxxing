Privacy Policy

Short version: I don't keep a database of your information. Requests go straight to my email, I use them to print and ship your order, and that's it. Here's the longer version.

what I collect

When you fill out the request form, you give me your name, email, quantity, shipping address, and a description of what you want printed. Depending on what you fill in, that may also include your phone number, a file (STL/OBJ/3MF/STEP/etc.), a color preference, or a budget.

how it's used and stored

  • The form submits directly to my email through Formspree, a third-party form service. I don't run my own database or CRM — your info lands in my inbox like a regular email.
  • I use it only to quote your print, make it, and ship it to you.
  • I don't sell it, rent it, or share it with advertisers.
  • The only outside party that sees any of it is the shipping carrier, and only the address needed to get your package to you.
  • If you send a file, I use it only to produce your print.

print time-lapses on social media

I record time-lapse video of my printer while it's working, including on customer orders, and post some of these to my social media (Instagram and similar). These videos show the printer and the model taking shape — not your name, address, or any other personal details.

If you'd rather your specific print not be filmed or posted, just say so when you submit your request and I'll leave it out.

cookies & tracking

This site doesn't use cookies, analytics, or ad trackers of its own. The only third-party service involved is Formspree, which processes the request form — see their own privacy policy for how they handle submissions in transit.

questions

If you want your info deleted from my inbox, or just have a question about any of this, message me on Instagram or reply to any quote email and I'll sort it out.
