Custom 3D printing for ideas, prototypes, and practical parts

Send a model or describe what you need. I review the details, confirm that the print is suitable, and email a clear quote before anything is charged. This independent FDM 3D printing service is built for useful one-off parts, prototypes, cosplay items, display pieces, and hobby projects.

Pricing is based on the variables shown in the quote process: filament used and print time. There are no surprise handling fees hidden inside the request form. A final quote is confirmed with you before production begins.

How it works

  1. Tell me what you want and upload an STL, OBJ, 3MF, or STEP file when you have one.
  2. I review scale, geometry, wall thickness, supports, and the likely print time.
  3. I email a quote. Nothing is printed or charged until you approve it.

What I’m printing on

The service uses a Flashforge Adventurer 5M with a 220 × 220 × 220 mm build volume, a standard 0.4 mm nozzle, and PLA-focused FDM settings. A part’s size, material, orientation, support needs, and layer-height choice can affect the final result.

STL files can contain scale or mesh problems. If you have preferred settings or a prepared G-code file, include them with your request so they can be reviewed alongside the model.

Request a print

Include enough detail for a useful first review: what the part is for, approximate dimensions, material preference, color, quantity, deadline, and shipping address. If you do not have a model, describe the idea and ask whether design help is possible.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a print cost?

The quote depends on filament used and print time. You receive a specific price after the file or description is reviewed, before production or payment.

Can I request a print without an STL?

Yes. Describe the object, its intended use, approximate dimensions, and any reference images. The feasibility of design work can be discussed by email.

What will you not print?

Requests for functional weapons, illegal items, or explicit sexual products are not accepted. Legal non-functional props, cosplay pieces, and display models may be reviewed.
