how it works

  1. Tell me what you want. Upload a file if you have one, or just describe it if you don’t.
  2. I look it over. I’ll check if it’s something I can print and figure out a fair price.
  3. I email you a quote. If you’re good with the price, I print it and ship it to the address you gave me.

what I’m printing on

printing policy

What I don’t print

I do not accept requests for:

  • Functional weapons or weapon parts.
  • Illegal items or items intended for illegal use.
  • Explicit or inappropriate items such as sex toys.

Non-functional replica props, cosplay items, and display pieces may be accepted as long as they are legal.

faq

I don’t have an STL file, can I still order?

Yeah, check the design box and describe what you want as best you can. I’ll get back to you about whether I can design it and what that’ll cost.

How much will it cost?

It depends on the size, filament, and print time. I’ll email you a specific number after I look at your file or description, and nothing gets charged until you say yes.

What files do you accept?

STL works best, but OBJ, 3MF, and STEP are fine too. If you have more than one file, zip them together.

Do you ship far?

I can ship wherever. I quote shipping based on the address you give me, so make sure it is complete and correct.
