Send me your idea: I’ll print it and mail it to you.

Hey — I run an independent 3D printing business from home. I create custom prints, prototypes, and hobby projects using my own equipment.

If you’ve got an STL file or just an idea, fill out the form with what you want and your address. I’ll look it over and email you back with a price and how long it’ll take before I print or charge you anything.

— thanks for checking this out

how it works

  1. Tell me what you want. Upload a file if you have one, or just describe your idea.
  2. I look it over. I’ll check whether I can print it and calculate a fair price.
  3. I email you a quote. If you accept it, I print the item and ship it to the address you provided.

my printer

I use a Flashforge Adventurer 5M. Here are its specifications:

  • Build volume: 220 × 220 × 220 mm
  • Nozzle: 0.4 mm; 0.25/0.6/0.8 mm available
  • Layer resolution: 0.1–0.4 mm
  • Maximum nozzle temperature: 280 °C; bed temperature up to 100 °C
  • Open frame, mainly set up for PLA
  • Maximum speed: 600 mm/s depending on the part

Note: STL files can have scale, geometry, or wall-thickness problems. Raw STL files are best; include G-code or your preferred settings if you have them.

printing policy

What I don’t print

I do not accept requests for:

  • Functional weapons or weapon parts.
  • Illegal items or items intended for illegal use.
  • Explicit or inappropriate items such as sex toys.

Non-functional replica props, cosplay items, and display pieces may be accepted as long as they are legal.

faq

I don’t have an STL file, can I still order?

Yes. Check the design box and describe your idea as precisely as possible. I’ll tell you whether I can design it and what it would cost.

How much will it cost?

The price depends on the size, material, and print time. I’ll send you a specific amount before charging you anything.

What files do you accept?

STL is best, but OBJ, 3MF, and STEP work too. If you have multiple files, send them as a ZIP.

Do you ship far?

I can ship anywhere. The cost depends on the complete address you provide.
